Introduction

Welcome to Tricky Tanks, a mobile application developed by William Baumgartner. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed in relation to your use of Tricky Tanks.

Information Collection and Use

We want to emphasize that we, as the developers of Tricky Tanks, do not collect any personal data from users of our app. However, please note that we use a third-party ad service called AdMob, provided by Google, which may collect certain information for advertising purposes as outlined below.

Information Collected By AdMob

AdMob is a third-party advertising service that serves ads within our app. When you use Tricky Tanks, AdMob may collect the following types of information:

  1. Coarse Location: AdMob may collect a unique identifier associated with your device (such as the IDFA on iOS) to help deliver tailored ads and track ad performance.
  2. Device ID: AdMob may collect your approximate location information (such as city or region) to deliver more relevant ads based on your general location. This data is not used to identify your exact location.
  3. Interaction Data: AdMob may collect information about how you interact with the ads displayed in Tricky Tanks. This could include interactions such as clicks, views, and installs.
  4. Crash and Performance Data: AdMob may collect information about app crashes and performance issues in order to improve the overall user experience.

Please note that the information collected by AdMob is subject to its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review their privacy practices for more information. You can find Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.
